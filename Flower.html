Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I care for my flowers to make them last longer?

To extend the life of your flowers, change the water every two days, trim stems at a 45-degree angle, remove any leaves below the water line, and keep them away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM local time. Delivery is available within a 15-mile radius of our shop. Additional charges may apply for express delivery.

Can I customize a floral arrangement?

Absolutely! Our talented florists can create custom arrangements based on your preferences. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your custom order.

What is your return policy?

We guarantee the freshness and quality of our flowers. If you're not completely satisfied with your arrangement, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a replacement or refund.

Flower Care Tips

Expert advice to keep your flowers fresh and beautiful

Watering Wisdom

Proper watering is essential for flower longevity:

  • Change water every 2-3 days
  • Use room temperature water
  • Add flower food to nourish blooms
  • Trim stems at an angle before placing in water

Light & Temperature

Create the perfect environment:

  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight
  • Maintain a cool room temperature
  • Avoid placing near heat sources or vents
  • Keep away from ripening fruit

Pruning & Maintenance

Regular care extends flower life:

  • Remove wilted blooms immediately
  • Trim stems every few days
  • Clean vase thoroughly between water changes
  • Remove any leaves below water line
